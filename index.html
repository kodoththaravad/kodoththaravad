Kodothu tharavadu(ancestral home) is one among the most celebrated aristocratic nair tharavads of the northern Malabar region; esteemed for its unparalleled cultural heritage and social standing A search for the roots of many such great tharavads of the region, takes us to travancore, Kochi, Kozhikkode or Karnataka... The historical evidences attests to the fact that the roots of the great Kodoth lineage sprouted from a place called Venneri, which is part of the present Malappuram district in Kerala. The geographical evidences of the glorious ancestry; that was destined later to travel to present-day Kasaragod, to take branches there, epitomizing and symbolising the later socio-cultural renaissance and progress of the new landscape; remains unscathed even today at venneri..
