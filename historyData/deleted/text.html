About

The main objective of this Kodoth Bhagavathi Devasthana Samrakshana Samithi is the General Admionistration of the Kodoth Bhagavathi Temople and related assets. This includes plan and conduct the auspicious days, festivals of the Tharavadu. observe daily Pooja, Archana, Kaliyattom, Pattulsavam or and other rituals. Also this committee is entrusted to plan and execute the construction and renovation works of the temples, Administration of the land assets etc. The Executive committee meets once every month and it conduct the annual general body of the members once in evbery year, unless any emergency arises. Treasurer of the Committee goes through the records carefully and see whether records and accounts are maintained properly and promptly . It supervises the work of all the employees of the temple. It lookss after various purchases of the commodities for the temple. Fiscal year of the committee is the calendar year
