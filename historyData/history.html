History

Architecturally : Puthiyya Veedu - means new house : it seems ( was) to be the oldest structure at Kodoth apart from the old Kodom temple. The architecture is karnatic architecture ( mostly resembles south Canara Brahmin architecture ) and in all probelity build around 500 to 600 years before . So the house is older than kodoth tharwad occupancy. I have heard Kodom rights were given or offered to be given on gold rights to Kodom tharavad to take care of Bhagavathi temple bu the family . Therefore in all probeblity that would naturally be the house the tharwad members occupied after the family left. Then the discriptions would follow the remaining names of houses : kizhakku Veedu ; padinchary Veedu etc. Architecture and nomenclatures are proofs of existential evidences . A story can be build along this lines. Puthiya Veedu has architectural evidences of being an unique house that was separate and older from any other architectural influences ( kerala thachu Sastha influences ) that later defined the present infrastructure which we call old architecture . Tin buildings ofcourse will be defined errors by future generations.
