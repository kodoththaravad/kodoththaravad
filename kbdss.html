About

In the partition deed of Kodoth Tharawad nearly one thousand acres were allotted to Kodoth Devaswsam. But almost all properties were lost since the tenants became the absolute owners of the property as per the Land Reforms Act. So, practically there were no income for maintenance of temple, Tharavad house and conducting poojas, Kaliyattam and other festivals. Moreover after partition three was no responsible person to look into these matters. Under this situation Adv.K.N. Kodoth(father of former Acting Chief Justice of Kerala, Justice V.P. Mohan Kumar and brother in law of former Chief Justice of Kerala Justice V.P.Gopalan Nambiar) took the initiative with the like minded members of the Tharawad and formed Kodoth Bhagavathi Devastapana Samrakshana Samithi (KBDSS). Now K.B.D.S.S is supervising all the functions..

The main objective of this Kodoth Bhagavathi Devasthapana Samrakshana Samithi is the General Admionistration of the Kodoth Bhagavathi Temple and related assets. This includes plan and conduct the auspicious days, festivals of the Tharavadu. observe daily Pooja, Archana, Kaliyattom, Pattulsavam or and other rituals. Also this committee is entrusted to plan and execute the construction and renovation works of the temples, Administration of the land assets etc. The Executive committee meets once every month and it conduct the annual general body of the members once in every year, unless any emergency arises. The audited accounts, reports, buget and plan for the forth coming year are presented for the approval of the general body in the annual meeting. Treasurer of the Committee goes through the records carefully and see whether records and accounts are maintained properly and promptly . It supervises the work of all the employees of the temple. and various purchases of the commodities for the temple. Fiscal year of the committee is the calendar year
